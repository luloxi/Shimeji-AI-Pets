# Shimeji Factory Proposal

**Shimeji Factory** opens intergalactic portals that deliver animated desktop companions. Users set an intention, artists craft a personalized shimeji, and the Chrome extension brings it to life across the web.

## The Problem

People want digital companions that feel personal and purposeful, not generic. Most experiences stop at static images or collectibles and never translate into daily utility.

## The Solution

Shimeji Factory delivers a simple ritual:

1. **Open a portal** with a Stellar wallet (Freighter)
2. **Set an intention** for the companion
3. **Receive a handcrafted shimeji** and watch it live on your screen

## Key Benefits

- **Daily utility:** Companions live with you as you browse
- **Personal meaning:** Intentions guide the art + behavior
- **Lightweight tech:** Stellar wallet connection and lean extension

## Product Components

- **Web App:** Portal reservation + intention input
- **Artist Workflow:** Crafting sprites and linking them to a portal
- **Chrome Extension:** Displays shimejis and syncs unlocks

## Go-To-Market

- Invite early adopters who love personalization
- Collaborate with illustrators and pixel artists
- Share short videos of shimejis living on real desktops

## Next Steps

- Finalize portal pricing + Stellar payment flow
- Launch a small batch of portals
- Publish the extension to the Chrome Web Store
